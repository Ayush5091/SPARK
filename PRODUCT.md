# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- Students: Seeking to discover campus events, participate actively, and verify points for their AICTE SPARK scorecard without manual paperwork.
- Coordinators/Admins: Managing slot capacities, publishing location-verified events, and reviewing student submissions efficiently.

## Product Purpose
SPARK digitizes and simplifies the tracking of AICTE activity points. It replaces manual logs with automated location-based (GPS) and time-bound photo verification, reducing manual verification workload while building a transparent, engaging record of student campus involvement.

## Brand Personality
Tech-native, Playful, Collaborative. Dynamic animations, custom-themed interactive cards, and vibrant feedback.

## Anti-references
- Complex corporate dashboards
- Monotonous, static SaaS template sites
- Dry, administrative form interfaces

## Design Principles
1. **Interactive Reward**: Event check-ins and point gains should feel visually satisfying, rewarding real-world participation.
2. **Transparent Clarity**: Give students and admins complete visibility into coordinate verification calculations and request pipelines.
3. **Collaboration and Community**: Elevate the campus community feel by showcasing live events and student milestones dynamically.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- Support `prefers-reduced-motion` defaults (instant transition fallbacks for animations).
- Proper color contrast levels for dark environments and color blindness.
